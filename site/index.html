A reading desk that respects your privacy.

OpenReader turns your computer into a focused document workbench. Open a PDF and everything you need—reading, annotation, search, comparison—is right there. No tabs, no toolbars, no distraction.

Every file stays on your machine. There are no cloud syncs, no telemetry, no accounts. OpenReader is built for people who treat their documents as private by default and want tools that do the same.

Everything you need. Nothing you don’t.

Built for the daily workflow of reading, marking up, and managing PDFs—without the overhead of a browser or subscription.

01

Read & Annotate

Highlight, underline, strikethrough, and add notes directly on the page. Copy with formatting preserved. Every annotation is local and yours.

02

Smart Search

Full-text search across open documents with instant results. Find what you need without leaving your reading flow.

03

Document Diff

Compare two PDFs side by side and see exactly what changed—word by word, page by page. Ideal for reviewing drafts and contract revisions.

04

Workspace Restore

Close OpenReader and come back exactly where you left off. Open documents, scroll positions, and annotations are restored automatically.

05

Local-First

No cloud, no telemetry, no accounts. The file never leaves your machine. OpenReader works entirely offline with zero network dependencies.

06

Cross-Platform

Native on Windows with the Microsoft Store. Experimental on macOS (Apple Silicon & Intel). Built with Python & PyQt.

AI-Native

Your documents,
your AI assistant.

OpenReader ships a built-in MCP server that lets AI agents like Claude, ChatGPT, and Cursor read, search, compare, and manage your PDFs directly—no cloud, no API keys, no data ever leaving your machine.

python -m pdfreader_lib.mcp_server
